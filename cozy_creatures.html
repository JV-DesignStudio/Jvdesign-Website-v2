Cozy Creatures: The Ultimate Snuggle

Work as a team to gather all the cozy items before the rain moves on!

1. Game Overview

This is a 1-4 player cooperative game where the Cozy Crew must complete a Snuggle Goal (by assembling Cozy Item Tokens) before the Rain Deck runs out, acting as the game's timer.

Attribute Details
Players 1 - 4 Cooperative
Goal Assemble the required Cozy Item Tokens in the Living Room.
Loss Condition The Rain Deck is depleted, or a "Sunshine Card" is drawn.

2. The Cozy Crew: Special Skills

Each player receives 3 Action Points (AP) per turn. Using a character's unique skill costs 1 AP.

3. Gameplay Actions (3 AP Limit)

Players can use their 3 AP to perform any combination of the following actions each turn.

Action Cost (AP) Details
Move 1 AP Move to an adjacent room on the board.
Search 2 AP Draw from the room's deck (Kitchen, Den, Storage) to collect an item or suffer a penalty.
Share 1 AP Give one Cozy Item Card to another player in the same room.
Assemble 3 AP Available only in the Living Room. Convert collected cards into a final Cozy Item Token.

4. Snuggle Goal Requirements

To win, the team must successfully Assemble all the required Cozy Item Tokens listed on the chosen Goal Card.

Goal Name Tokens Required (Win Condition) Cards to Assemble (Example Total)

Reading Marathon 1 Stack of Books, 1 Snack Platter 3 Chapter Books, 1 Snack Platter Base
The Ultimate All 3 Items 1 Blanket, 1 Pillow, 3 Chapter Books, 1 Hot Cocoa

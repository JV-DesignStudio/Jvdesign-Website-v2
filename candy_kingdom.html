The Sweetest Way to Play

Core Concept: Teamwork and Sweet Smarts

Forget complicated rules and combat‚Äîsuccess is all about teamwork, creative ideas, and solving silly, sweet challenges. This is a simple Tabletop Role-Playing Game (TRPG) designed to encourage collaborative problem-solving.

How to Solve a Problem (The Dice Roll)

When faced with a challenge, you roll a single six-sided die (d6) using one of your three main stats. A roll of **4, 5, or 6 is a SUCCESS!**

  • **Chewiness:** Strength, pushing, smashing, and toughness.
  • **Fizzyness:** Speed, jumping, hiding, and agility.
  • **Sweet Smarts:** Solving puzzles, riddles, and remembering clues.

Adventure 1: The Tangled Candy Cane Forest

The mischievous **Sour Patch Kid** has twisted the straight candy cane trees into a messy maze! The heroes must get through to help the Grumpy Cloud find its way back home and restore the candy rain.

Part 1: The Sticky Floor

The path is covered in gooey, sticky caramel. A Gummy Bear is stuck!

Rolls to Allow:

  • Fizzyness Roll: Leap quickly from safe spot to safe spot.
  • Sweet Smarts Roll: Find a secret path of crunchy toffee pieces.
  • Chewiness Roll: Push a bridge or smash a path through the goo.

Part 2: The Rock Candy Blockade

A solid, sparkling wall of rock candy blocks the path completely!

Rolls to Allow:

  • Chewiness Roll: Smash through a weak spot in the rock candy.
  • Sweet Smarts Roll: Figure out a magical chant or recipe to make the candy soft.

Part 3: The Flavor Riddle

Two large candy canes stand at the end. One is sweet (correct path), one is sour (a trap).

The Riddle:

"I have no mouth, but I can tell you a story. I'm not a book, but I have many words. What am I?"

Solution: A Gummy Bear!

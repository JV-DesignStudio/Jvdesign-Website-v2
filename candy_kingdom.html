Development Updates

November 5, 2025

New Core Hero: The Cotton Candy Bard

We've added the Cotton Candy Bard to the roster, giving players access to the **Fluffy Distraction** ability, perfect for diverting the Sour Patch Kid's minions!

October 15, 2025

Refining the Caramel River Crossing

Playtesters found the first puzzle too difficult. We've introduced more **Sweet Smarts** opportunities for finding the toffee path, making the start of the adventure smoother.

September 25, 2025

Core Stat System Lock-In

After extensive testing, the **Chewiness, Fizzyness, and Sweet Smarts** stat system has been locked for the final game document. It provides the perfect balance of simplicity and character distinction!

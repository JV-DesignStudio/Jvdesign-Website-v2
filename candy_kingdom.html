Welcome to the Sweetest Place on Earth

The Candy Kingdom is a land of wonder where trees are made of lollipops, the ground is soft like cake, and the rivers flow with chocolate milk! It's usually a land of joy, but a dark shadow—or rather, a sour shadow—has fallen.

The Naughty Sour Patch Kid

The mischievous Sour Patch Kid has used his pranks to twist the sweet world sour! From making the royal moat sticky with caramel to creating a confusing maze of candy cane trees, it's up to your brave candy heroes to solve his riddles and bring the fun and flavor back!

The Core Mechanic: Simple & Fun!

When a hero faces a challenge (like a Sticky Floor! or a Rock Candy Blockade), they roll one six-sided die (d6) to see if their clever idea works.

4, 5, or 6: SUCCESS! The idea works perfectly!
2 or 3: PARTIAL SUCCESS! It works, but a small, silly complication happens (e.g., they get sticky!).
1: UH OH! The plan fails this time. Time for a new idea!

Adventure 1: The Tangled Candy Cane Forest

1. The Sticky Floor

The forest path is completely covered in gooey, sticky caramel. A poor little **Gummy Bear** is stuck right in the middle! Heroes must find a way to cross without getting completely gummed up.

  • Fizzyness Roll: Leap quickly from safe spot to safe spot.
  • Sweet Smarts Roll: Find a secret path of crunchy toffee pieces.
  • Chewiness Roll: Push a bridge or smash a path through the goo.

2. The Rock Candy Blockade

A solid, sparkling wall of rock candy blocks the path completely! It's too high and hard to climb. The heroes need a clever plan to get past this crystalline obstacle.

  • Chewiness Roll: Smash through a weak spot in the wall.
  • Sweet Smarts Roll: Figure out a magical chant or recipe to make the candy soft.

3. The Flavor Riddle

At the end of the maze, two large candy canes stand side by side. One is sweet (the correct path), and one is sour (a trap)! The only way to find out which is which is to answer the final riddle left by the Sour Patch Kid: **"I have no mouth, but I can tell you a story. I'm not a book, but I have many words. What am I?"**

(Hint: The answer is an item found in the Candy Kingdom!)

FANTASY CARD GAME

The Card of the Rings: Quest for the Crown

A competitive two-player card game of resource management, artifact collection, and epic quests, where the first player to achieve 5 Victory Points claims the crown.

Core Mechanics & Features

  • ★ Quest-Driven Victory: Be the first to earn 5 Victory Points (VP) by completing one of the three available Quests each turn.
  • ★ Build Your Party:Recruit Companions like the Captain of the Guard and Elf Archer to gather enough Power to fulfill Quest requirements.
  • ★ Collect Artifacts: Gain powerful Permanent Artifacts (e.g., Ring of Courage, Shield of Ages) that grant ongoing bonuses and tactical advantages.
  • ★ Resource Management:Carefully manage your Gold Cards in your Bank to ensure you can pay the cost to Recruit new Companions and Artifacts.

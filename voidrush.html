Void Rush: Endless Runner Prototype

Dodge, destroy, and power-up your way to a high score!

Controls & Power-Ups:

Desktop: Movement: **Left/Right Arrow Keys** or 'A' / 'D' | Shoot: **Spacebar**, **Control** Key, or **Click**

**Mobile:** Movement: **Touch and hold** the **left or right half** of the game screen. | Shoot: **Tap** the screen.

🛡️ SHIELD (Green):

Absorbs one hit, saving a life.

⚡ RAPID FIRE (Blue):

Greatly increases your firing speed.

⭐ SCORE MULTIPLIER (Yellow):

Doubles the score earned for each hit.

⏱️ TIME SLOW (Purple):

Slows all enemies for a short duration.

💥 BOMB (Orange):

Instantly clears all enemies on screen.

❤️ LIFE BOOST (Red):

Restores one life (up to 5 max).

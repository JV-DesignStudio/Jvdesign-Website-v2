Void Rush: Endless Runner Prototype

Dodge and destroy the obstacles in your path!

Controls:

Desktop:

Movement: **Left/Right Arrow Keys** or 'A' / 'D'

Shoot: **Spacebar**, **Control** Key, or **Click** the Mouse

Mobile:
Movement: **Touch and hold** the **left or right half** of the game screen.
Shoot: **Tap** the screen (fires automatically while moving).

Goal: Shoot the obstacles to increase your score and avoid collisions to save your lives!
